Milestones

John highlights significant milestones in his life, including growing up with his wife, Suzy, and raising their three children. His career journey has been diverse, with points at Disney, Knott's Berry Farm, Hollywood production stuntwork, and architecture with his firm, Hesmahaulch. John acknowledges that his life lacked a fixed plan, but he embraced the uncertainty, adapting to evolving wants and needs.

John's eclectic career path includes jobs ranging from mechanic, ICEE truck driver, and theme park attractions to stuntwork, art instruction, and more. Demonstrating an aversion to feeling trapped in a single career trajectory.

John expresses pride in his role as a father, emphasizing the importance of imparting his values to his children. He acknowledges the influence of his own father and strives to pass on life lessons and philosophies.
